Next one is Sweden!

King's name is King Carl XVI Gustav, he is 77 years old (Birth in 1946). He is married to Queen Silvia. They have three children: Victoria, Carl Philip and Madeline. All of their children are married and have children.

Here is one interesting fact about Victoria and Carl Philip. So now Victoria is Crown Princess and Carl Philip is only Prince. But when Prince Carl Philip was born he was named Crown Prince (Because the first male in the family should inherit the throne), but there came new rules. So at the age of 13, Victoria was once again heir to the throne.

Because, here, mostly, I am showing one outfit of each Queen/future Queen. Here, I will show one of Crown Princess Victoria.
